02 Privacy by design

De-identification, built for tabular data

IDENTIFIERS
Direct IDs
Names, MRNs and account numbers removed or replaced with surrogates.
DATES & RARE
Quasi-identifiers
Dates shifted and rare values generalized to block re-identification.
KEYS
Stable pseudonyms
Consistent surrogate keys — so de-identified rows still join across sources.
Built in-house for structured clinical data, then operated in production — privacy that preserves the join. ⋈
